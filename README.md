# Apna Whisky Club
Whisky Club Website
## About
Our Whisky Club exists to celebrate the craft, culture, and camaraderie of whisky. Our purpose is to:  
🔍 Explore the world of whisky through guided tastings, distillery visits, and themed events.  
🗺️ Connect members through collaborative tasting sessions held 4 times each year.  
🧠 Educate with tasting notes, flavour profiles, and whisky lore—blending fun with insight.  
💬 Celebrate with friends from our walking family and beyond , creativity, and the joy of discovery—whether you're a seasoned sipper or curious newcomer.  
We’re not just a club—we’re a spirited collective where every sip tells a story, and every member adds a verse.
